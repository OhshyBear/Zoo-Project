Africa World

Africa World is a wonderful place to experience the different habitats of Africa without all the dangers. We have many different exhibits featuring several different animals from the Savanna to the Sahara.

Under the Sea Aquarium

Under the Sea Aquarium is home to over 1200 different species of Ocean life. Come here to relax and enjoy the serenity that ocean life brings. From sharks to feather dusters people young and old are sure to find something fasinating in this exhibit.
