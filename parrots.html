Parrot Training

Parrot Training is a special event where zoo guest get to interact with and handle the parrots. Guests will get to wear a special glove and hold a delicious treat for the parrots to fly up to them and eat. Their claws and beaks sharp and strong so we will have a safety briefing to properly handle them. Age restriction to 8 yrs and older.

Open Every Day at 3 PM

Ages 8 and up!
