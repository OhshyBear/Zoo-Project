Feed the Lorikeet's

Join us in feeding the Lorikeet's

Feeding the lorikeets is a special event where visitors get up close and personal with the lorikeets! Zoo staff will escort visitors into the lorikeet pins where we will handout special containers with food in them for the lorikeets. The lorikeets will land on your head, shoulders and arms in order to eat the tasty treats presented to them. Fun for all ages so come one come all for a wonderful experience!
